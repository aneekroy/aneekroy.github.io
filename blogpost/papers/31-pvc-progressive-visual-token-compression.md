# PVC: Progressive Visual Token Compression

**Authors:** Yang et al.  
**Venue:** CVPR 2025  
**Paper ID:** 31

## Abstract

Unified compression for images and videos. Progressive encoding with AdaLN layers. Reduces tokens per image from 256 to 64 while maintaining performance.
