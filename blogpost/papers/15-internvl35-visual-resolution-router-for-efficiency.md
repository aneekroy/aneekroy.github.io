# InternVL3.5: Visual Resolution Router for Efficiency

**Authors:** Wenhai Wang et al.  
**Venue:** arXiv 2025  
**Paper ID:** 15

## Abstract

Visual Resolution Router (ViR) dynamically adjusts visual token resolution. Decoupled deployment separates vision/language across GPUs. Achieves 4.05x inference speedup.
