# Eve: Efficient VLMs with Elastic Visual Experts

**Authors:** Rang et al.  
**Venue:** AAAI 2025  
**Paper ID:** 28

## Abstract

1.8B parameter model with adaptable visual expertise. Balances linguistic and multimodal capabilities. Outperforms 7B LLaVA-1.5 in multimodal accuracy.
